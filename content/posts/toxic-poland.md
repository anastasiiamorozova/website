We automated 3,000 **FOIA requests** to map illegal waste dumps across Poland, then used an **LLM** to extract location data from **PDF responses**. Ground-truthed on site, the dataset revealed nearly 200 hazardous dump sites nationwide — three of them caught fire in May 2024 alone.
